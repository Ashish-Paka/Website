Publications & Research

Academic contributions to the field of robotics and autonomous systems

2023

Distributed Task Allocation in Heterogeneous Multi-Robot Systems

Paka, A., Johnson, M., & Smith, R.

IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)

This paper presents a novel approach to task allocation in heterogeneous multi-robot systems using distributed consensus algorithms. The proposed method demonstrates improved efficiency in dynamic environments while maintaining robustness to communication failures.

PDF Code Presentation
2022

Reinforcement Learning for Adaptive Navigation in Complex Environments

Paka, A., Garcia, L., & Thompson, K.

Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS)

We introduce a reinforcement learning framework for robot navigation that adapts to changing environmental conditions. Our approach combines model-based and model-free techniques to achieve both sample efficiency and adaptability in previously unseen scenarios.

PDF Code
2021

Human-Robot Collaboration in Shared Workspaces: Safety and Efficiency Considerations

Johnson, M., Paka, A., & Chen, Y.

ACM/IEEE International Conference on Human-Robot Interaction (HRI)

This study examines the safety and efficiency tradeoffs in human-robot collaborative tasks. We propose a framework that dynamically adjusts robot behavior based on human proximity and intent, resulting in improved task completion times while maintaining high safety standards.

PDF Video
2020

Vision-Based Pose Estimation for Robotic Manipulation in Cluttered Environments

Paka, A., & Davis, T.

IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)

We present a robust vision-based approach for estimating object poses in cluttered environments. Our method combines depth information with semantic segmentation to achieve accurate pose estimation even under partial occlusion, enabling reliable robotic manipulation.
