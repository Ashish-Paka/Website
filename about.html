About Me

Robotics & Autonomous Systems Engineer passionate about advancing human-robot collaboration.

Background & Vision

As a Robotics & Autonomous Systems Engineer, I am driven by the vision of creating intelligent robotic systems that can seamlessly collaborate with humans. My journey began with a strong foundation in mechanical engineering and has evolved to encompass advanced robotics, AI, and autonomous systems.

Currently pursuing my M.S. in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at Arizona State University, I focus on developing innovative solutions for human-robot collaboration, multi-robot systems, and industrial automation. My research interests lie at the intersection of reinforcement learning, computer vision, and motion planning.

With experience spanning mechanical design, robotics research, and autonomous systems development, I bring a unique perspective to solving complex engineering challenges. I am particularly passionate about creating robots that can understand, adapt to, and enhance human capabilities in various domains.
